# Privacy Policy for RC Telemetry Monitor

**Last Updated: 28-06-2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how RC Telemetry Monitor handles your information. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### 1.1 Bluetooth Data
The App requires Bluetooth permissions to connect to your RC radio transmitters or telemetry modules. 
* **Purpose:** To receive real-time telemetry data (Voltage, RSSI, GPS, etc.).
* **Storage:** Bluetooth device names and MAC addresses are stored locally on your device to facilitate automatic reconnection. This data is **never** transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

### 1.2 Location Data
On certain versions of Android, access to the device's location (Fine or Coarse) is required by the system to perform Bluetooth scanning.
* **Purpose:** Solely to enable Bluetooth connectivity.
* **Tracking:** The App **does not** track your physical location, record your history, or share your location data.

### 1.3 Telemetry Data (GPS, Voltage, RSSI)
The App parses telemetry data provided by your RC equipment, including GPS coordinates of your RC model.
* **Processing:** All telemetry data is processed in real-time on your device for display purposes.
* **Sharing:** This data is **never** uploaded, shared, or sold. It remains strictly on your device.

## 2. Local Storage
The App uses local storage (Android DataStore) to save your dashboard configurations, widget layouts, and model settings. This data remains on your device and is deleted if you uninstall the App.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cloud storage providers. We do not share any information with third parties.

## 4. Security
The security of your data is important to us. Since all processing happens locally on your device, your data is protected by the standard security measures of the Android Operating System.

## 5. Children’s Privacy
Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
